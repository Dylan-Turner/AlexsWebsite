Debating


This publication will run every week during the official ACTDU debating seasons. It will include tips from experienced debaters and a compilation of debating resources for each division’s weekly theme or topic.

Tune in every Saturday to receive a curated reading list, an explanation of useful terminology and debating techniques as well as example arguments written by some of Canberra’s best young debaters.

This can be used as a refresher for experienced debaters as well as a helping hand for beginners!

Good luck debaters.
